# What is Networking?

Networking is the process of connecting two or more devices so they can communicate and share information.

Examples of devices:

- Computers
- Servers
- Mobile Phones
- Routers
- Switches
- Printers

Without networking, devices would operate independently and could not exchange data.

---

## Real World Example

When you open www.google.com:

1. Your laptop sends a request.
2. The request travels through your home router.
3. Your ISP forwards the request.
4. Multiple network devices route the request.
5. Google's server receives it.
6. A response is sent back.

All of this happens because of networking.

- Cloud computing
- File sharing
- Video conferencing
- Online gaming
- Distributed systems

---

## Types of Networks

### LAN (Local Area Network)

A network within a small area.

Examples:

- Home WiFi
- Office network

### MAN (Metropolitan Area Network)

A network covering a city.

Example:

- University campus networks

### WAN (Wide Area Network)

A network spanning large geographical areas.

Example:

- Internet

---

## Components of a Network

### Host

A device participating in communication.

Examples:

- Laptop
- Server

### Router

Connects different networks.

### Switch

Connects devices within the same network.

### Firewall

Filters traffic based on rules.
